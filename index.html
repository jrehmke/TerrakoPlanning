  • About foam clay

    • The price of Model Magic is approx. 2 cents per gram, while foam clay is about 5 cents per gram, just through Amazon. While the difference does add up, for a relatively small project like Terrako it would be better to go with the higher quality product. Only the best for our egg boi! Besides, Hobby Lobby sells 250 grams of foam clay for around $13, and that's without the 40% off coupon.
